1. Information We Collect

Bakwaas FM collects minimal information necessary to provide and improve the audio streaming service. This may include:

  • Basic usage analytics (such as screens viewed, session duration)
  • Technical device information (such as device model, OS version, and crash logs)
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information unless voluntarily provided by the user (e.g., support emails sent to us)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and improve app performance
  • Fix technical issues
  • Ensure content is appropriate for users aged 13+
  • Understand general usage trends in a non-personal way

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. We do not share location data, advertising identifiers, or analytics data with external partners except basic anonymous analytics provided by Apple or Google.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use standard third-party tools for analytics or crash reporting (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics or Apple’s Analytics). These tools only collect anonymous, aggregated technical data.

  • We do not use any third-party advertising SDKs that collect personal data.
  • We do not share device location, Bluetooth information, or contact information with any partners.

5. Children’s Privacy

Bakwaas FM is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to access, delete, or request correction of the information we hold. You may contact us to submit such requests.
